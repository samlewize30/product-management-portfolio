# Product Life Cycle

## Product Lifecycle
- Introduction
- Growth
- Maturity
- Decline

## Product Management Lifecycle
1. Conceive
2. Plan
3. Develop
4. Qualify
5. Launch
6. Deliver
7. Retire

## Key Deliverables
- SWOT & PEST analysis
- Product roadmap
- Business case
- Marketing strategy
- Launch plan
- Beta testing
- End-of-life plan

## Responsibilities of a Product Manager
- Define product vision
- Conduct market research
- Build product strategy
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams
- Monitor product performance
- Gather customer feedback
- Prioritize features
- Manage product throughout its lifecycle

## Key Takeaways
- Product Lifecycle describes the stages a product goes through.
- Product Management Lifecycle describes the process of managing a product from idea to retirement.
- The Product Manager owns the product from conception to retirement.
