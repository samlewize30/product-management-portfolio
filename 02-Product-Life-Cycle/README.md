# 5️⃣ RACI Framework

## Meaning of RACI

| Letter | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| **R** | Responsible — Performs the work |
| **A** | Accountable — Owns the outcome |
| **C** | Consulted — Provides input and expertise |
| **I** | Informed — Kept updated on progress |

### Purpose

- Clarifies ownership.
- Prevents confusion.
- Improves communication.
- Keeps projects organized.

# 💭 My Reflection

One of my biggest takeaways from this module is that Product Management goes far beyond building products.

A Product Manager serves as the bridge between customers, engineering, design, marketing, sales, executives, and other stakeholders to deliver products that solve real-world problems.

Success depends on:

- Strategic thinking
- Effective communication
- Cross-functional collaboration
- Continuous customer feedback
- Data-driven decision-making
- Managing the product throughout its entire lifecycle
