# Day 1 - Introduction to Product Management

## What I Learned

Today I started the IBM Product Management Professional Certificate.

### Key Concepts

- Product Management is the process of planning, developing, launching, improving, and retiring products.
- The goal is to create products that solve customer problems while delivering business value.
- Product Managers work with customers, designers, engineers, marketing, sales, and executives.
- Product Managers create product vision, strategy, roadmaps, and prioritize features.
- Customer research is essential to building successful products.
- Product Managers use personas to understand users and make informed decisions.
- Product Management differs from Project Management because it focuses on the product strategy rather than project execution.

## Personal Reflection

Today's lesson helped me understand that Product Management is more than managing software development. It is about identifying customer problems, creating valuable solutions, and guiding products throughout their lifecycle.
